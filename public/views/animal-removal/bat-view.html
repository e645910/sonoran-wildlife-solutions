Animal control (wildlife control and removal) benefits both humans and wildlife, allowing us to live together in our desert habitat. SONORAN WILDLIFE SOLUTIONS specializes in bat removal and control services in Northwest Tucson, Oro Valley, Dove Mountain, Catalina, Saddlebrooke, and the surrounding areas.

We offer the following services:

  • immediate removal of a bat inside your home
  • property inspections and structure inspections and identification of roost sites
  • exclusion of bats from your home or other structures
  • information and educational presentations
