On occasion, a home or building may have a Woodpecker problem or a rock dove (pigeon) problem. although we can exclude specific areas of your home such as chimneys and porch beams, we do not try to trap and relocate birds. after all, they are birds and will simply fly back

Putting a plastic or ceramic owl on your roof to scare away woodpeckers and rock dove (pigeons) only works in the short run, if it works at all. woodpeckers and rock dove (pigeons) simply habituate to (become comfortable with) the plastic owl and learn to ignore it .

Sadly, at some point you may have a dead animal on your property. if you do, your first clue will usually be the smell. decaying flesh has a very strong odor. dead animals may be found on your property for many reasons. a larger animal may be hit by a car and limp onto your property before it dies from internal bleeding. a smaller animal may become trapped in your attic or under your house and die from a lack of food and water, or from someone else putting out poison.
